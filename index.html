About Us

Welcome to the musical world of Goran (Goya) Gojevic and Ivan (Vanilla) Radovanovic —where the rich traditions of Balkan music come alive. Goran is a seasoned clarinetist and saxophonist and Ivan is an experienced harmonicist and accordionist—both renowned for their captivating performances that celebrate the vibrant cultures of South-Eastern Europe. With nearly 40 years of experience under their belts, they bring an authentic blend of artistry and professionalism to every event.

Goran and Ivan specialize in providing unforgettable music for:

  • Concerts and Entertainment Venues
  • Private Parties
  • Weddings & Special Occasions

Why Choose Goya & Vanilla

Goran and Ivan’s extensive repertoire focuses on the rich and diverse traditions of South-Eastern European Balkan music, including Serbian, North Macedonian, Greek, Romanian, and Bulgarian styles. They tailor their performances to match the theme and mood of your event, ensuring a personalized and authentic experience. Goran and Ivan are a musician duo that play with accompaniment tracks; however, they work with several live ensembles and bands as needed.

Goran and Ivan’s performances bring the cultural heritage of the Balkans to life, making your event truly unforgettable.
